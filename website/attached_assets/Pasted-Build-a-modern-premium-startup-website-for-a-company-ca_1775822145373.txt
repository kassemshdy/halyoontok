Build a modern, premium startup website for a company called **Halyoon**.

## About Halyoon
Halyoon is a **data-driven social growth engine** that helps people, brands, and media businesses build social presence and grow successful channels. It analyzes viral content patterns across platforms like **TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels**, classifies channels and influencers, detects trends, and uses AI to generate new videos based on proven styles, categories, prompts, and performance patterns.

Halyoon is not just an AI video generator. It is a full system for:
- collecting and structuring social trend data,
- discovering what works,
- recommending the best viral patterns,
- generating new content with AI,
- and continuously improving through feedback loops.

## Key product positioning
Halyoon’s main service is to allow any person or business to build a strong social media presence and grow successful channels using data, recommendation systems, and AI-generated content.

## Important use case
**Tfole Kids** is the first use case powered by Halyoon.

Tfole Kids is a **child-safe short-video feed app** that uses Halyoon’s trend intelligence, recommendation engine, and AI content generation system to deliver safe, engaging, educational, and entertaining short videos for children.

## Website goal
Create a website that presents Halyoon as a serious **AI + media-tech + growth infrastructure** startup. The website should feel:
- modern
